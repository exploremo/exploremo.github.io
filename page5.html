Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line which is available 24/7.

What information should I include in my travel inquiry?

Please include your travel dates, number of travelers, destination preferences, budget range, and any special requirements. The more details you provide, the better we can tailor our response to your needs.

Do you charge fees for consultation services?

Initial consultations are always free. For complex custom itineraries, we may charge a planning fee which is fully deductible from your final booking cost.

Can I visit your office in person?

Absolutely! Our office is open Monday-Friday from 9AM to 6PM. We recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure a travel specialist is available to assist you.
